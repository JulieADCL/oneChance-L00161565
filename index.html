One Chance is a simple dice game where players take turns rolling a six-sided die. The goal is to be the first player to reach 100 points or more.

  • Players take turns rolling a six-sided die.
  • Each turn consists of one or more rolls of the die.
  • If a player rolls a 1, their turn ends, and they score no points for that turn. Play passes to the next player.
  • A player may choose to stop rolling at any time. The score is the sum of all the numbers rolled on that turn.
  • The turn score is then added to the total score.
  • The first player who reaches a score of 100 or higher wins.
